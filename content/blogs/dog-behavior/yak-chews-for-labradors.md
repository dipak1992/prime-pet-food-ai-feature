# Yak Chews for Labradors

Labradors are famous for enthusiasm, food motivation, and strong chewing habits. That makes them a great candidate for Himalayan yak cheese chews, but it also means sizing matters. A Lab that chews with urgency should not be matched to the smallest possible chew.

Prime Pet Food yak chews are a strong fit for Labs that need a longer-lasting chewing outlet, especially after exercise or during indoor downtime.

## Labrador chewing behavior

Many Labs chew because they enjoy food, texture, and activity. Under-enriched Labs may become mouthy, steal objects, or destroy softer toys. A dense chew gives that energy a better job.

## Best size for Labs

Most Labradors should start with a large or extra large yak chew depending on weight and chewing style. If your Lab destroys toys quickly, size up. If your Lab is gentle, a large chew may last across several supervised sessions.

Use the estimator before buying: [How long will this last for my dog?](/pages/how-long-will-this-last-for-my-dog)

## Best routine

Offer a yak chew after a walk, fetch session, or training. This helps your Lab shift from high energy to focused decompression. Remove the chew when the session ends and store it for the next supervised routine.
