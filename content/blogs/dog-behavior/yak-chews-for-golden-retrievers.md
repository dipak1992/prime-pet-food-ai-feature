# Yak Chews for Golden Retrievers

Golden Retrievers are social, food-motivated dogs that often love having something to carry, mouth, and chew. A durable chew can be especially helpful after activity, during evening downtime, or when your Golden needs a calm enrichment outlet.

Prime Pet Food Himalayan yak cheese chews can work well for Golden Retrievers because they are dense, long-lasting, and better suited to supervised chewing than quick treats.

## Golden Retriever chewing behavior

Goldens are usually moderate to persistent chewers. They may not always chew with the same intensity as the strongest power chewers, but their food motivation can make them focused. That means the chew should be large enough for safe supervision and durable enough to stay engaging.

## Best yak chew size

Most Golden Retrievers should start with a large yak chew. Very strong chewers or larger males may do better with extra large. Gentle Goldens may use one chew across multiple short sessions.

## Best routine

Use yak chews after walks, training, or play. This helps transition your Golden into a calmer state. Remove the chew before it becomes small enough to swallow, and turn the leftover nub into a puff treat.
